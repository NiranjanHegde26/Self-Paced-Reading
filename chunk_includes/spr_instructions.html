Task Instruction

Dear Participant,
Thank you for choosing to continue with the experiment.

Your task in this experiment is to read sentences. These will be shown to you piece by piece. First, you must press ENTER to start reading the sentence. At first, you'll see dashes representing the length of the sentence:

___ ___ ____ __ ___ ____

To read the first part of the sentence, press the SPACEBAR:

Alex ___ ____ ____ ___ ____

As soon as you press the SPACEBAR again, the next sentence part will appear, and the previous part will disappear. This will continue until you reach the end of the sentence. After reading the last part of the sentence, press the SPACEBAR to continue.

___ can ____ ____ ___ ____
___ ___ deal ____ ___ ____
___ ___ ____ with ___ ____
___ ___ ____ ____ any ____
___ ___ ____ ____ ___ man

Read the sentences at your usual speed and make sure you understand the meaning. Please always keep one finger on the SPACEBAR while reading.

Some sentences are followed by the comprehension question that refer exclusively to the sentence that you just read. These will be simple yes/no questions. Please press D key for "yes" and K key for "no". After you have answered, the question will disapper. However, there is no minimum time to answer the question.

You will have a few practice trials to get familiar with the task. After the completion of practice trials, the actual experiment will begin.

This task will take approximately 10 minutes to complete. If you have any queries or concerns regarding the task, please contact nsatyanarayana@lst.uni-saarland.de

Click the "Next" button below when you are ready to start the practice trials.
