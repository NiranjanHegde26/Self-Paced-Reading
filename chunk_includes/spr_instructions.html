Task Instruction

Dear Participant,
Thank you for choosing to continue with the experiment.

In this task, you will read sentences in English. These will be displayed to you word by word. First, a cross mark (X) will appear in the middle of the monitor. To start reading the sentence, press the SPACE BAR. Before beginning the next sentence, you will have to press the ENTER key.

Read the sentences at your own normal reading speed so that you understand the content. Please keep one finger on the SPACE BAR while reading. Always press the SPACE BAR to see the next word. Please note that you cannot go back to the previous word.

Some sentences are followed by the comprehension question that refer exclusively to the sentence that you just read. These will be simple yes/no questions. Please press D key for "yes" and K key for "no". After you have answered, the question will disapper. However, there is no minimum time to answer the question.

You will have a few practice trials to get familiar with the task. After the completion of practice trials, the actual experiment will begin.

This task will take approximately 10 minutes to complete. If you have any queries or concerns regarding the task, please contact nsatyanarayana@lst.uni-saarland.de

Click the "Next" button below when you are ready to start the practice trials.
